Gas Day September 23, 2001
Gallup Compressor Station will be doing run testing.  Capacity for San Juan will stay at 
800,000 mmbtu/d.

Gas Day September 24, 2001
Gallup Compressor Station at full load.  Capacity for San Juan will increase to 850,000 
mmbtu/d. and Mainline Thoreau West System will increase from 1010,000 to 
1090,000 mmbtu/d.

Posted to the Transwestern Web Site to the customers Sunday Morning September 23, 2001
