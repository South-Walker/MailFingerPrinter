Come Meet Enron Kids' Center's New Executive Director, Lorraine Gibbs. 
Drop by EBC2 on Thursday, August 2, 2001 between 5:30 P.M. and 6:30 P.M. or on Tuesday, August 7, 2001 between 11:30 A.M and 12:30 P.M. to meet Lorraine Gibbs, Enron Kids' Center's newly hired Executive Director. 
Lorraine comes to Enron Kids' Center after working as the Executive Director of the Knowledge Beginnings Back-up Childcare Center in downtown Houston. Lorraine has devoted the past ten years of her life to the childcare and development profession.
Come learn more about Lorraine and how she and her staff will be helping your child become ready for school. View the floor plans for the childcare center and find out the latest on the construction schedule.
Have a question about the Knowledge Beginning's philosophy? Want to know about the qualifications of the staff who will be taking care of your child? Still trying to decide if the Center is right for your child? Just curious about the programs the Center will offer? Lorraine will be able to answer all of these questions and more.
Spaces for each session are limited so please RSVP to Charla Stuart by emailing her at <mailto:charla.stuart@enron.com> or call Charla at X36202. We look forward to seeing you there!!!
