Enron is pleased to announce that Lorraine Gibbs will be joining us as the Executive Director of the new Enron Kids' Center.  Lorraine comes to Enron Kids' Center with ten years of experience in the childcare and development industry. She was previously the Executive Director of the downtown Houston Knowledge Beginnings back-up childcare facility.  Lorraine is a graduate of UCLA where she received a Bachelor of Science Degree.  From there she went on to receive her Childcare Management Program Certification from North Harris/Montgomery College. 
Please join us in welcoming Lorraine.
